• Determination of branch currents in presence of both the sources:
      Select both the switches, S1 and S2 to power.
      And then click on Simulate.


  • Determination of branch currents in presence of V1 source only:
      Select the switch S1 to power and S2 to short.
      And then click on Simulate.


  • Determination of branch currents in presence of V2 source only:
      Select the switch S1 to short and S2 to power.
      And then click on Simulate.
